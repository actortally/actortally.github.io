Actor Tally

A private, 100% on-device tally of which performers appear in the videos you watch.

No account · no servers · no tracking. Everything you log — entries, photos and settings — is stored locally in your own browser and never leaves your device.

What it does

  • Keeps a personal, local tally of performers, with photos, tags and star ratings.
  • Up to 4 independent pages / workspaces, each with its own data and layout.
  • Optional PIN that encrypts your data at rest.
  • Statistics: top performers, activity over time, by-site breakdowns, badges, yearly recap.
  • One-click local backup & restore. Available in 7 languages.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

Actor Tally does not collect, store on any server, sell, or transmit any personal data. The extension works entirely on your device.

  • What is stored: the performer names, sites, video titles, photos, tags, ratings and settings you choose to log — saved only in your browser's local storage on your computer.
  • What is accessed: only the current tab's title and URL (and, if you ask, a thumbnail) at the moment you click “Log”. Nothing runs in the background and no other tabs are read.
  • What is sent: nothing. Actor Tally makes no network requests and contains no analytics or trackers.
  • Sharing: your data is never shared with us or any third party. Backups are files you export yourself and keep on your own device.
  • Control: you can delete any or all of your data at any time from within the extension.

Because no data ever reaches us, we have no user data to access, correct or delete on your behalf — it is entirely under your control locally.

Actor Tally is an independent tool. It does not host, display or provide any video content; it only records tallies you enter yourself.

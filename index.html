Actor Tally How it works Privacy FAQ

Your log. Your device. Nobody else's.

Actor Tally is a private tally counter for people who watch a lot of video. One click logs who you just watched. Over time, you get a personal analytics dashboard nobody else can see — because, by design, nobody else can.

Free · No account · No ads · Zero network access — verifiably.

Not "we don't look." Can't look.

Most apps ask you to trust a privacy policy. Actor Tally doesn't ask you to trust anything — the extension contains no code that can connect to the internet. No server, no account, no analytics. Every entry lives in your browser's local storage on your device, and there is no mechanism by which anyone — including the developer — could read it.

Verifiable, not promised

Anyone technical enough to open the extension's code can confirm there is no network call in it. That's the entire architecture.

Locked, if you want it

Set a PIN and your data is properly encrypted at rest (PBKDF2 + AES-256-GCM via your browser's built-in Web Crypto) — not just hidden behind a lock screen. A keyboard shortcut instantly hides the dashboard.

Yours to keep

Full export and import, a 30-day recoverable trash, multi-step undo. Your history moves to a new computer as a file you carry — not an account someone hosts.

No dark patterns

No guilt-trip streaks, no fake scarcity, no notifications. Because it can't connect to anything, there are no ads and never will be.

Log someone in three seconds

1 · CLICK

On any video page, click the icon. It reads the page — title, cast links, metadata — and suggests likely names, ranked by confidence.

2 · TAP

Tap the right name, or several. Not listed? Start typing and it autocompletes from everyone you've logged before. Optional star rating and tags.

3 · DONE

One tap logs it, with a running count. You never leave the page. A manual picker handles sites that defeat automatic detection.

A real analytics dashboard

Everything you log feeds a full dashboard in its own tab:

  • Leaderboard of your most-logged people
  • Calendar heatmap of your busiest days
  • Activity over time and a per-site breakdown
  • Personal records — biggest day, biggest month
  • Badges and milestones as your collection grows
  • Cards, not rows — each person with count, rating, tags, photo
  • Everything clickable — filter the whole history by tag, name, or site
  • Yearly recap — a shareable visual summary of your year

Questions

Is it really free?

Yes — fully free, nothing gated, no trial, no premium tier. If it's useful, there's an optional donation link below. Donating changes nothing about what the tool does.

Where exactly is my data?

In your browser's local extension storage, on your device. Nowhere else. There is no cloud copy — so back it up with Export if it matters to you.

What if I forget my PIN?

Your data is encrypted with keys derived from your PIN, on your device. Nobody can recover it — not even the developer. Keep an exported backup somewhere safe.

Does it slow down my browser?

No. It does nothing in the background — it runs only in the moment you click it.

How do I verify the zero-network claim?

Open your browser's extension page, inspect the source, and look for any network call — fetch, XHR, WebSocket, beacon. You won't find one. Or watch the network panel while using it: silence.

Keep it free, keep it yours

Actor Tally has no ads and no premium tier — on purpose. If it earns a place in your browser, you can support development. The Telegram channel is also where updates are announced — the extension itself will never notify you of anything, because it can't.
